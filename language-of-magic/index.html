The rune of death will kill the next person it touches after activation, unless it is touched by a rune of life. If so, both will become deactivated.

The rune of fire burns as long as the owner can hold up. If it is touched by a rune of water, it will deactive in a cloud of steam.

Rune of water

The rune of water lets out a massive blast of water. If touched by a rune of cold, the water become ice. If touched by a rune of fire, it deactives in a cloud of steam.

Rune of life

The rune of life can reanimate anything, under the owener's command. For dead bodies, the consciousness of the dead person will conjoin with consciousness of the owner, therefore having the dead person's voice and intellect inside their head.
